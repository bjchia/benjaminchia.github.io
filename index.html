Lotus Edge Heart Valve

Medical Devices

Aortic stenosis, the calcification of the native aortic heart valve, is the leading cause of open-heart surgery. The valve narrows and blocks the facilitation of blood throughout the rest of the body.Recent breakthroughs have given rise to the inception of transcather aortic heart valve replacements (TAVRs), a minimully-invasive procedure that replaces that native valve.

I'm working on the mechanical braid components, testing the durability of both the whole valve and individual metal parts.
