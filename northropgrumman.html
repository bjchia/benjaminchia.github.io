Northrop Grumman-Missile Ejection Manufacturing

SUMMER 2019 || Skills: Mechatronics, Project Management || URL: Columbia Class Submarines

I interned with Northrop Grumman during the summer of 2019, working on missile launching technology used on Columbia class submarines. I joined the launcher manufacturing team to assist with pre-production support and project management of critical deadlines.

My responsibilites included:

  • Spearheading the development of the annual comprehensive manufacturing plan for relevant stakeholders
  • Implementing new control schematics to increase operator safety and testing efficiency for an underwater missile launch test fixture.
  • Conducting tool design and research on part sourcing for a vertical welding machine.
