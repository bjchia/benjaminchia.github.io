Boston Scientific-Mechanical Heart Valve Testing

SUMMER 2018 || Skills: SolidWorks, 3-D Printing, Mechanical Testing, Rapid Prototyping || URL: Lotus Edge Heart Valve

I worked on a class of medical devices that are alternatives to the traditionally invasive process of open heart surgery: transcatheter aortic heart valves (TAVI). I worked directly with the exploratory team on failure characterization and exploratory designs for next-gen. improvements.

My responsibilites included:

• Leading the structural reliability and durability testing of the device on the exploratory team.

• Designing a new 29mm valve seal from initial drawings, Solidwork solid-body modeling, to prototype manufacturing.

• Designing a custom test fixture in durability testing, reducing seal-to-frame slippage by 70% of original distance.

• Assisted in component-level seal tensile strength tests, conducting tests on 15+ manufacturing methods.
